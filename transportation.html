Getting to HKUST

HKUST is located at Clear Water Bay, on the Sai Kung Peninsula.

From Hong Kong International Airport

  • Red taxi (Hong Kong International Airport → HKUST)
    Estimated travel time: 45 minutes
    Estimated cost: HK$375
  • Airport Express (Hong Kong International Airport → Kowloon Station) + Red Taxi (Kowloon Station → HKUST)
    Estimated travel time: 25 minutes + 30 minutes
    Estimated cost: HK$105 + HK$160
  • Bus Number A29 (Hong Kong International Airport → Po Lam) + Taxi (Red or Green) or Bus 91M (Po Lam → HKUST)
    Estimated travel time: 100 minutes + 15 minutes
    Estimated cost: HK$44 + HK$70 or HK$6.8

From Hong Kong West Kowloon Station

  • Red taxi (Hong Kong West Kowloon Station → HKUST)
    Estimated travel time: 35 minutes
    Estimated cost: HK$165
  • MTR (Hong Kong West Kowloon Station → Hang Hau Station) + Taxi (Red or Green) or Minibus Number 11M (Hang Hau Station → HKUST)
    Estimated travel time: 40 minutes + 15 minutes
    Estimated cost: HK$13.5 + HK$45 or HK$6

Public Transport to HKUST

  • Public Transportation System in Hong Kong
    • Mass Transit Railway (MTR): https://www.mtr.com.hk
    • The Kowloon Motor Bus (KMB): https://www.kmb.hk
    • Citybus & New World First Bus (NWFB): https://www.citybus.com.hk

The MTR (subway) stations closest to HKUST are Choi Hung, Diamond Hill, Hang Hau and Po Lam. There are various bus and minibus services available for getting to campus.
